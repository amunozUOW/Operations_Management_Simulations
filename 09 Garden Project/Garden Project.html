You manage a community garden growing pumpkins for the annual Harvest Festival, now 16 weeks (112 simulated days) away. Each plot moves through six sequential phases — planning → soil prep → planting → growing → inspection → harvest. Your job is to allocate the right gardeners to the right activity at the right time so every plot is finished before the festival, on budget.

Task durations are not fixed — they depend on who does the work. Higher-skilled gardeners finish faster but cost more, and each gardener can only work one plot at a time. Pause any time to re-assign gardeners (even busy ones — their plot returns to the queue).

Key rules
  • Volunteers cannot work without an Experienced or Master gardener supervising them.
  • Soil Prep needs an Experienced or Master gardener; Inspection needs a Master.
  • Growing takes a fixed 10 days — no staff required.
  • Watch the queue badges and the chart to spot bottlenecks, then re-allocate to clear them.
