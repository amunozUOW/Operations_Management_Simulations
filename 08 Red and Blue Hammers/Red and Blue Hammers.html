Hammer Shop Simulation

You manage a retail shop selling two products — red and blue hammers — to customers who arrive at random. Each minute you balance staffing, inventory ordering, and customer satisfaction.

  • Staff: regular serve 1 customer/min at $5/min; emergency serve 2 customers/min at $25/min.
  • Inventory: red ($15 cost, $20 order fee, $1/min holding); blue ($25 cost, $200 order fee, $2/min holding). Orders arrive 2 minutes after issue.
  • Demand: red 1–4 customers/min buying 1–2 each (3-min patience); blue 1–2/min buying 1–4 each (2-min patience).
  • Quality (customer satisfaction) blends fill rate and wait time into one score: stockouts reject customers (red 25%, blue 50%) and waits beyond a customer’s patience erode it — so keep both products stocked and served quickly.

Runs for 60 minutes. Fails if cash falls below −$1,000, or satisfaction stays below 40% for 5 consecutive minutes. Maximise profit while keeping customers satisfied.
