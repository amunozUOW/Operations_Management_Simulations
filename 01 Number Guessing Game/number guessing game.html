Your First Decision as Operations Manager

The company is launching a brand-new product today and you need to decide how many units to stock. The problem? You have zero demand data. All you know is that demand is somewhere between 1 and 200 units. No spreadsheets, no forecasts, just your judgement. Make a guess, read the feedback, and refine. The fewer guesses it takes you, the better.

▶ How to Play
  1. Enter a number between 1 and 200 in the input box below.
  2. Click Submit Guess (or press Enter).
  3. Read the feedback. It tells you whether to go higher or lower, and by roughly how much.
  4. Keep refining until you find the exact number.
